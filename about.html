The Person Behind The Method

Hi, I'm Mads.

I'm a Pilates instructor, a mom, and someone who's learned — over and over — that movement is medicine for both body and mind.

METHOD by mads was born from my own journey toward movement with intention. It's not about perfect form or a perfect body. It's about showing up on the mat, in whatever shape that day takes, and trusting that consistency beats perfection every time.

My slogan is "Thoughts in Motion" — because that's exactly how I live. We figure it out as we go. We keep moving. And somewhere in that motion, we become who we're meant to be.

What I Stand For

Movement with intention

Three threads that run through everything I teach — on the mat, in the studio, and in life.

☉

Show Up

Mat Pilates that meets you where you are — building strength, mobility, and body awareness without the pressure to be perfect.

◆

Stay Present

The mental side of the practice matters just as much. Breath, focus, and self-compassion are part of every session.

❄

Keep Moving

Real bodies, real schedules, real life. Progress isn't linear — the goal is just to keep showing up for yourself.

"You don't need to have it all figured out. You just need to start moving — the rest follows."
— Mads
